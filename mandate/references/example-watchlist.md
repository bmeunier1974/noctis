# Example watchlist — high-volatility US names

A few tickers a high-risk mandate might steer toward. Illustrative only; the agent may
discover its own names to satisfy the mandate.

- NVDA — mega-cap semiconductor, wide daily range on AI-cycle news.
- TSLA — high-beta autos, headline-driven, heavy intraday volume.
- AMD — semiconductor peer, momentum-prone around earnings.
- SMCI — small-cap AI-server name, extreme volatility.
- COIN — crypto-correlated, gaps hard with BTC swings.
